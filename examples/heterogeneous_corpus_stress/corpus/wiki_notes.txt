Onboarding checklist
====================

New employees need an identity account, a laptop asset tag, VPN enrollment, and
access to the team documentation space. Contractors receive time-limited VPN
access and must confirm the end date with their sponsor.

Incident escalation
===================

Urgent incidents are escalated to the on-call lead. Include impact, affected
service, first detected time, and whether customer data may be involved.

Incident communication
======================

For customer-visible incidents, send a status update every thirty minutes until
the service is stable. The message should include scope, mitigation, and next
expected update time.
