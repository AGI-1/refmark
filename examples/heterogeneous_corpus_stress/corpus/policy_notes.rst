Data Retention Policy
=====================

Customer account logs are retained for thirteen months. Security incident logs
are retained for twenty-four months. Deletion requests do not remove records
that must be kept for fraud, audit, or legal-hold obligations.

Access Review Policy
====================

Managers review privileged access every quarter. Any entitlement without a
current business owner is suspended until ownership is confirmed.

Legal Hold
==========

When legal hold is active, normal retention schedules are paused for the named
records. The legal team must explicitly release the hold before deletion
continues.
