Release Window

Production releases happen between 09:00 and 15:00 UTC. Changes outside that window require incident commander approval and a rollback owner on call.

Rollback Plan

Rollback plans keep the previous image available for twenty-four hours. Operators should verify database migrations are reversible before deployment starts.

Token Rotation

Service tokens rotate every 90 days after replacement credentials are deployed. Expired tokens are disabled only after health checks confirm the new credentials.

Audit Retention

Audit logs are retained for 180 days by default. Finance and regulated customer workspaces can extend retention to one year by policy override.
